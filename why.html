Why: Because sharing ideas is good. Because sharing ideas that can be made into the things is cool.

The dream is that 3-D printers will be as omnipresent as regular printers are now. In the dream, we encourage the sharing of designs for *things*, the so-called solutions for the problems of the day. Hopefully, this helps stimulate the tinkering impulse, the inventive spirit.

Let us end the tyranny of the thousand custom plastic parts.
