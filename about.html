About, motivations, etc

You're at a construction site in the boonies. A plastic gismo breaks off your surveying gear. What do you do? Drive back one hundred miles to the supplier only to find he's closed for the day? Or pull out your reprap (http://reprap.org/bin/view/Main/WebHome) machine and make the part in fifteen minutes?

Kevin Griffin is in the process of making a proto-type machine to show investors and prospective clients. He has tested motors and electronics, rejecting some and choosing others.

This is an open source hardware company. Anything that might be an improvement to the design, will be noted and posted.
